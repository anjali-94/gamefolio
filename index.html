About me

Past

Architect and project manager by education. I have worked on multiple real estate projects of many different scales in many different roles - from architect to project manager on both contractors and clients side.

Present

Recently I have finished 3W Academy Lithuania web developer course, during which I have tried several front and backend technologies and I have found that Javascript "suits" me quite well. Because of this I studied it on my own and made (still making more) several smaller projects. This does not mean that I am only interested in Javascript.

Future

Development process interestes me as a whole so I would like to find a position where I would be able to work with new products, maintain existing ones or event software development when I gain enough experience.
